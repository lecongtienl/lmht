DIANA

SCORN OF THE MOON

MOONSILVER BLADE

Every third strike cleaves nearby enemies for an additional magic damage. After casting a spell, Diana gains Attack Speed for her next 3 attacks.

MOONFALL

Diana reveals and draws in all nearby enemies and slows them. If Diana pulls in one or more enemy champions, the moonlight crashes down onto her after ashort delay, dealing magic damage in an area around her, increased for each target beyond the first pulled.
